About

I've spent more than ten years at the intersection of AI and design, shaping how people experience intelligent technology.

At Microsoft, I helped embed intelligent features directly into Windows. At Amazon, I shaped core Alexa experiences and designed a voice interface for astronauts aboard NASA missions to the moon. My most recent work focused on prototyping new ways for people to interact with and work alongside AI.

That background gives me a practical perspective on what AI can actually do today, where it falls short, and how to close the gap. I bring the same hands-on, user-centered thinking to my consulting and speaking work: helping organizations move past the hype and start getting real value from AI tools.
