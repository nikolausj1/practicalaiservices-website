OVERVIEW

The AI Assistant for City of Sartell provides an intelligent, AI-powered solution that enables city employees to instantly access policies, ordinances, and procedures through Microsoft Teams. Built on Microsoft Copilot and GPT-4, this system delivers accurate, cited answers in 2-4 seconds empowering staff to make informed decisions while working in the field.

The Challenge

  • City employees spend significant time searching through PDF documents for policy and ordinance information
  • Field staff must call back to city hall for answers, creating interruptions for office staff
  • Information retrieval is slow and inefficient, particularly for mobile workers

The Solution

An AI-powered assistant integrated directly into Microsoft Teams (desktop and mobile) that provides instant access to city policies, ordinances, and procedures. Employees simply ask questions in plain English and receive accurate, cited answers within seconds—no searching, no calling, no delays.

HOW IT WORKS

The system delivers answers in four simple steps:

1

Ask a Question

Employee opens Microsoft Teams on phone or desktop and types or speaks a question:

"What's the parking time limit on residential streets?"

↓
2

AI Processes Request

The system searches your city's ordinances, policies, and procedures using advanced AI technology (GPT-4 or GPT-4o) to find relevant information.

↓
3

Receive Cited Answer

Within 2-4 seconds, the employee receives an accurate answer with citation:

"Residential streets in Sartell have a 24-hour parking limit per City Ordinance 123.45, Section 3.2."

↓
4

Access Full Document

If more detail is needed, a link provides instant access to the complete ordinance or policy document.



User Experience Examples

Scenario 1: Parking Enforcement

User Input: "Can residents park RVs on their front lawn?"

System Output: "No, recreational vehicles (RVs) cannot be parked on front lawns in Sartell. Per City Ordinance 94.15, Section 2(B), RVs must be parked on approved driveways or in rear yards. Violators may be subject to a $100 fine for first offense. [View Full Ordinance]"

Scenario 2: Noise Complaint

User Input: "What time does the noise ordinance start on weekends?"

System Output: "On weekends (Friday and Saturday nights), the noise ordinance is in effect from 11:00 PM to 7:00 AM per City Ordinance 130.04, Section 3(A). During weekdays, the quiet hours are 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM. Excessive noise during these hours may result in citations. [View Full Ordinance]"
